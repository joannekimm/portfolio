Overview

With the recent escalation of COVID-19 cases, it had become apparent that people aren't fully educated on the number of cases in their area, so my team and I decided to base our hackathon project on COVID-19 hotspot trackers. Amidst the growing concerns and fear of transmission within the community, we thought it was important for users to know which stores have been affected by COVD-19 in real time. We decided it was best to create a Google Chrome extension which shows directly on the map, if stores have reported cases of coronavirus.

Brainstorming

Prototype

Our team used a bold red colour to emphasize the COVID-19 alert cues on the map and hover overlays to indicate the stores that have been affected recently.
